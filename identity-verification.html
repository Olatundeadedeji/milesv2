Identity verification is the process of confirming the identity of an individual or organization. At Miles, we work with you to establish trust and ensure that the person or organization is who they claim to be. At Miles, we help you conduct a background check on your employees or partner using the best practices. Through our identity verification service, you can prevent fraud by ensuring that the person or organization attempting to access sensitive information or make a transaction is who they claim to be.

If certain security compliance is required to verify the identity of a customer or employee as part of regulatory compliance, we can come to your rescue. For example, financial institutions are required to follow Know Your Customer (KYC) regulations, which involve verifying the identity of their customers.
