How we work

Miles drivers provide drivers by partnering with businesses that need drivers on a temporary or long-term basis. Miles Drivers is responsible for recruiting, hiring, and managing the drivers, while the business that contracts with us is responsible for providing the vehicles and setting the schedules and routes for the drivers.

Miles Drivers have a pool of qualified drivers who are available to work on an as-needed basis, or we handle the entire recruitment process for the drivers. Miles Drivers is responsible for handling compliance and regulatory issues related to the drivers, such as obtaining commercial driver's licenses and maintaining records of drug and alcohol testing. Miles Drivers typically charges a fee for their services, which may be based on a flat rate or on a per-driver basis(subject to contractual agreement). Miles charge additional fees for specialized services such as training or compliance support. The business that contracts with Miles is responsible for paying these fees, in addition to any expenses related to the drivers' salaries and benefits.
